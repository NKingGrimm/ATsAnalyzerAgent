

Descripción larga

As Embedded Software Engineer in Capgemini you will design, develop, and deliver high-quality embedded software solutions for automotive Electronic Control Units (ECUs), applying Automotive SPICE (ASPICE) best practices across the full software development lifecycle, for an automotive project in Querétaro. 

Your Role

    Develop and integrate embedded software components in C/C++ for automotive ECUs and advanced systems (ADAS, Park Assist, Instrument Clusters, Vision Systems).
    Analyze software requirements and define architecture and design in compliance with ASPICE standards.
    Perform bench, integration, and system testing; support unit and module-level validation activities.
    Diagnose and resolve software issues in development, production, and customer environments.
    Collaborate closely with customers and global teams to ensure technical alignment, quality, and on-time delivery.

Your Profile

    Solid experience in embedded software development using C or C++, preferably in automotive environments.
    Knowledge of AUTOSAR (BSW and application layer) and automotive communication protocols (CAN, LIN, Ethernet, UDS).
    Hands-on experience with GIT and requirements management tools such as IBM Rational DOORS.
    Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills in cross-functional settings.
    Bachelor’s degree in Electronics, Electrical, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
    You should be living in Querétaro or willing to relocate: this is an on-site position on a hybrid scheme. 

Nice to have: Experience with AUTOSAR configuration tools (EB Tresos, Vector DaVinci), Embedded Linux/Yocto, QNX, or Hypervisor environments.
